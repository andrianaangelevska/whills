This guide describes some of the green flags we look for profiling a prospect at Novvum.

> If you have any problems or requests, please contact Raj or Rohit.

### Here are some traits we love to see in a client.

- Expert or accolades in the industry
- Successful past software projects
- Understanding of agile process and mvps
- Reasonable timeline and budge
- A clear and well thought out set of specs or documentation
- Company or investor backed
- In revenue or established user base
